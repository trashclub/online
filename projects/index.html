Strange Objects

What is the most eco-friendly way to throw away a strange object? By strange, we mean objects that don’t belong (or would be harmful) in a landfill but also don’t fit into the category of cardboard, plastic, paper, and metal of curbside recycling. Are there creative ways to reuse this object if it can’t be recycled? By researching ecologically friendly solutions of for discarding a strange object, we may discover an unexpected process to reusing or recycling an object.

Composting

Pre-pandemic, Trash Club had the composting bin out on the 4th floor of 370 Jay St during our Wednesday snack days. We would then drop off the compost bin at Borough Hall’s Greenmarket on Thursdays. Given the remote situation we are currently in, Trash Club is figuring out other ways to support composting efforts.
