Trash Club serves as a community platform for ongoing investigation and involvement related to present-day processes for managing waste in New York City and beyond. With our cohort of trash-enthusiasts, we hope to gain a deeper understanding of the successes and failures of waste systems, and feel empowered to imagine alternatives to the current cycles of use, ruin, and salvage.
